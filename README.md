Business Workflow: ABS Prospectus "Plain English" Translator

Workflow: Simplifying highly technical legal and financial disclosures from Asset-Backed Securities (ABS) prospectus documents.

User: Non-technical executive leadership and external stakeholders who need to understand risk and performance without parsing dense financial jargon.

System Input: A technical paragraph or "Risk Factor" section from a prospectus (e.g., descriptions of cash flow waterfalls, credit enhancement, or asset eligibility criteria).

System Output: A concise, 2-3 sentence "Plain English" summary that highlights the core meaning and potential impact.

Value: Automating the first pass of these summaries saves significant time for the audit and legal teams and reduces the risk of misinterpretation by stakeholders who are not subject matter experts.
